Stainless Steel Bag Filter

Stainless steel bag filter is a professional liquid filtration equipment, it can effectively remove the liquid in different sizes of particles, so as to achieve liquid filtration, purification, separation, recovery purposes. Bag filter is mainly composed of three parts: filter shell, mesh blue and filter bags. When the liquid is filtered through the bag filter, the liquid enters from the side of the filter vessel or below the inlet, and is blown into the filter bag from the filter bag supported by the net blue. The filter bag is expanded by the impact of the liquid and the uniform pressure surface so that the liquid material The entire surface of the filter bag to be evenly distributed through the filter bag of liquid along the metal support network blue wall. Discharged from the bottom of the filter outlet. The filtered particulate impurities are trapped in the filter bag to complete the filtration process.
